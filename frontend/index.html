About ResumeFIT

ResumeFIT is an innovative job portal developed by Group 155 at VIT Bhopal University for our second-year project exhibition. Using Natural Language Processing (NLP) technology, ResumeFIT recommends job listings tailored to your skills and career preferences.

Whether you're a job seeker looking for the right opportunity or an employer posting vacancies, ResumeFIT ensures a seamless connection with AI-powered insights and personalized recommendations.

Our Key Features

AI-Based Job Recommendations

Leveraging NLP models, ResumeFIT matches your skills, experience, and job search criteria to suggest the most relevant jobs.

Employer Job Postings

Employers can post job listings, and our system will ensure that the right candidates see the right opportunities.

Seamless User Experience

Simple, user-friendly interfaces for job seekers and employers, making job applications and job postings easier than ever.
